Click on the the Get Started button above to visit the Kanopy Streaming collection. Start by Signing up for your personal Kanopy account. Click on the Sign Up link at the top of the page.

Enter your name & email address and choose a password, then click on the Sign up button.

Open your email and look for the verification message from Kanopy.

Click on the Verify my email button in the message. You will return to the Kanopy website.

Kanopy needs to confirm your Sutherland Shire Libraries membership; click on the Add library card button.

Enter your full library card number (14 digits) with no spaces and click on Save. You are now ready to start watching Kanopy films!
