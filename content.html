Content Page - Freegal

Using Freegal, library members can download up to 3 free MP3 songs per week from the Sony Music catalogue. Choose from thousands of artists and hundreds of thousands of songs, all free and legal.

3 Simple Steps to Get Started

  1. Login to Freegal with your library card number
  2. Search the collection and preview songs
  3. Click on Download Now - the song is yours to keep, forever!

Download Free Songs

These files do not expire and can be transferred to iPods and MP3 players or burnt to CD. (did we mention It's legal too!)
