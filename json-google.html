Current Bulk Loans

A bulk loan is a box of books or other items in a language other than English, borrowed from another library service for a short time. This is a free service. To request a bulk loan ask at your nearest library.

Bulk loans in these languages are available now or are "on order" at branches of the Sutherland Shire Libraries.
