RB Digital Magazines

Download digital editions of popular magazines and read them on any device - PC, Mac, iPad or Android tablet.

Sutherland Shire Libraries have partnered with RB Digital to provide access to a collection of over 100 magazine titles that library members can download for FREE.

Troubleshooting

Having trouble accessing Borrowbox?

When you are ready, click the Start Troubleshooting button below. Read the instructions carefully and follow the process described. When you have completed each step return here and answer the question for the next troubleshooting step.

Start Troubleshooting

Open Borrowbox in a web browser (not the App) and try to login using your barcode number from your library card as the Library ID and the last 4 digits of the barcode as your PIN.
