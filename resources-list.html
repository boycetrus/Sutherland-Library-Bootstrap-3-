Australian Public Affairs Full Text

APA-FT is an online collection of scanned images of articles from more than 560 journals covering Australia’s political, economic and social affairs.
Subjects include: environment, globalisation, history, industrial relations, immigration, politics, social policy, public health and national identity.

Designed to meet every student's science research needs, this database contains full text for hundreds of science encyclopedias, reference books, periodicals, and other sources.
